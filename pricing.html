Frequently Asked Questions

Is VoxCMD really 100% free?

Yes, the core VoxCMD system is completely free and open source under the MIT license. You can use it for personal or commercial projects without any cost. We only charge for custom development work, specialized integrations, and priority support services if you need them, but these are entirely optional.

What's included in the open source version?

The open source version includes everything you need for a fully functional voice command system: offline voice recognition, an extensive library of development and system commands, IDE integrations, and cross-platform support. It's a complete solution that works right out of the box for most developers' needs.

When would I need custom development services?

You might consider our custom development services if you have specific requirements such as: integration with proprietary software, industry-specific command sets, enterprise deployment across large organizations, compliance with specific security requirements, or if you need white-labeled versions of VoxCMD for your clients or products.

How much do customization services cost?

Customization costs vary depending on the scope and complexity of your requirements. We provide personalized quotes after an initial consultation to understand your specific needs. Our pricing is transparent and project-based, so you'll know exactly what you're paying for. For a general estimate, please contact our team with details about your project.

Can I modify VoxCMD myself instead of hiring you?

Absolutely! As an open source project, you're free to modify VoxCMD in any way you see fit. Our documentation includes guides for extending and customizing the system. Our custom development services are simply an option for organizations that prefer to have expert assistance or lack the internal resources to implement complex modifications.

Do you offer support for the open source version?

The open source version is supported by our active community through GitHub issues and our Discord channel. Many questions and issues can be resolved quickly through these channels. For guaranteed response times or dedicated support, we offer premium support packages as part of our custom services.
